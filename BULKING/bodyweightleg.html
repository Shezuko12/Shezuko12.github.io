BARBELL-SQUAT

Barbell-squat is a compound exercise that activates muscle group throughout your lower body, including your hamstrings, glutes, and lower back muscles. Perform barbell squats by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart.

To Perform:

  • Safely load the barbell onto your traps and shoulders. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out, core braced, and chest up.
