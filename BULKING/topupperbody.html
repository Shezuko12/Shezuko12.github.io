Top Upper-Body Exercises

BICEPS CURL

The Biceps curl is a highly recognizable weight-training exercise that works the muscles of the upper arm, and to a lesser extent, those of the lower arm. It's a great exercise for seeing results in strength and definition.

To Perform:

  • Stand straight with a dumbbell in each hand, your feet shoulder-width apart, and hands by your sides.
  • Squeeze the biceps and lift the dumbbells.
  • Once the dumbbells are at shoulder level, slowly lower the arms to the starting position.
  • Repeat.
